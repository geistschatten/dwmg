Mobile Services

Consumers are browsing, searching and buying more via mobile devices. With mobile internet use growing at a rapid pace, our Mobile Marketing services have grown in demand and are becoming a must-have with many businesses. DWMG makes it easy for your business to implement the newest and most impactful marketing platform today.

Mobile Websites

With more than 70 million Americans browsing websites using mobile phones every month, it’s a necessity for your business to have a mobile-friendly website. People perform searches on their mobile phones for a purpose, and 9 out of 10 searches result in an action – placing a phone call, visiting a business, or making a purchase.

It is essential to give your customers a quick and easy way to call your business and find your location. Convenient one-touch features like Click-to-Call and Mobile Maps take full advantage of mobile phone technology. DWMG will make your website mobile-friendly; delivering your website’s content more effectively for mobile visitors.

QR Codes

QR Codes are easy to use and an ideal compliment to all forms of advertising and marketing. No need for searching or typing! Customers just scan it, and the information you want to deliver loads on their phone.

DWMG will generate your custom QR Codes, set up the response actions, and help you implement this extremely versatile mobile marketing tool. The best part is QR Codes are effective and very affordable.

Most of our clients receive a 20-30% redemption rate on text coupons and offers!

DWMG will help your business harness the power of text messaging, and implement Text Marketing as part of your strategy.
