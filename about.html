About Us

Direct West Media Group, Inc. provides the tools and direction businesses need to grow. Our clients depend on our decades of experience to deliver innovative advertising methods and implementation of effective marketing tools.

Our focus is to offer premium-marketing services at affordable price points to start-up businesses and companies seeking to gain greater share in competitive markets.

Our internal processes are based on our expertise in delivering the highest quality advertising and marketing. We have developed our services for efficiency and effectiveness; aiming towards quick turn-around and keeping costs affordable for businesses of all sizes.

We offer a robust selection of methods to promote your business, and will cater them to meet your needs and achieve your goals. DWMG will guide you through entire process and will ensure your marketing efforts are properly launched, tracked and maintained.
